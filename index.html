Students who do not reside on campus are usually discomfort to travel distance only to cast their vote if they do not have any lecture activities and if they do, spend a lot of time on campus and in ques to execersis their franchase. Candidate, break down their pocket for election campaigns such as posters, hiring of PR systems, and teams and others just to win. The institution also have nose diave their finances, they spends a lot of finances to provide materials for elections such as papers, printing ballots, election office etc, in some cases state institutions responsible for elections are hired to organize the election at a fee. These difficulties inspired me to develop campus elect, a system that gives solutions to any form of elections on campus.
